ClickFunnels Review 2020 | Pros, Cons, Features

Introduction to ClickFunels

Clickfunnels provides you tools and strategies at one place to help your business grow with promotions and landing pages. 

With the help of ClickFunnels, you can build awesome landing pages, quick sales funnels that convert customers for you, Smart Shopping carts in just 1 click, email marketing, and Facebook marketing automation at the same place.

With ClickFunnels, you can generate new leads that you can connect with, and also create simple funnels that capture your visitor’s contact information and their messages so that you can generate new leads to follow-up with again and again also after they leave your web page.

If you are in touch with the world of digital marketing, you should have at least heard about ClickFunnels, the landing page builder that was helping online sales funnels. In this review, we will take you deep into the Pros and Cons to ClickFunnels if it is legitimate business marketing software or not.

Clickfunnels has created itself as a pretty big name since it started, as it was one of the largest marketing software as a services company. I think it is no surprise why people love Clickfunnels as it is all about making money for its users. More specifically, it’s about giving business software that helps them to turn their website’s visitors into customers and then conversions. There is no wonder ClickFunnels has grown so much and all-time higher in the list of landing page builders and marketing software in such a short period of time.

In this review, we will go through the gist of Clickfunnels like what it is, the positives and the negatives, the features, customer service, and more. I am making this review on the basis of my own experience using ClickFunnels’ startup plan which itself includes coreClickFunnels features and other special and unique features.

ClickFunnels Review Highlights

Here is a list of ClickFunnels’ review highlights on which we are going to know more in this review:

  1. ClickFunnels is a nice landing page builder, with an easy drag and drop editor.
  2. Very Easy and various integrations that allow you to capture emails and take payments inside the ClickFunnels.
  3. Built with A/B Testing capabilities.
  4. Easy to learn and use.
  5. Focused on making its users more money.
  6. They offer a free trial of two weeks.

What is ClickFunnels?

Clickfunnels started in 2014, but in just 3 years it’s been in the words of Forbes, “silently revolutionizing the online marketing industry.” With ClickFunnels, it makes you easier for businesses and entrepreneurs to sell online using this with the ease of complicated marketing technology, which is called sales funnels.

Clickfunnels Founded by Russell Brunson who is as famous as his creation, and for a good reason. He’s made a fortune not just only for ClickFunnels, but other marketing adventures. He played around with email marketing and eventually began to master sales funnels.

ClickFunnels started out as a way to help Brunson and his team to create funnels more quickly for different ventures as usually, it takes over a week or more to develop funnels. So they realized their creation can be useful to others and then they have launched ClickFunnels as its name means just click and make your funnels it works also in just some steps only.

What Are Funnels? How Sales Funnels Work?

In digital marketing, it is a process that can take people who don’t know you and convert them as your customers, which is known as a marketing funnel. In e-commerce, sales funnel is a series of steps designed to guide visitors to the checkout page.

Step1: Imagine a funnel.
Step2: Imagine this funnel will be helpful for your website visitors.

Your site visitors are looking around by checking things out. And with a sales funnel you can get your website visitors to the right place for what they actually want to purchase.

ClickFunnels is created to save your time and energy like if you want to create funnels for yourself and develop it for yourself then it requires a lot of technological proficiency and also maybe some other third-party applications, so it this respect ClickFunnels Succeeds.

What ClickFunnels can do for you?

Pros of ClickFunnels

Makes you money

One of the best things about ClickFunnels is its focus on making money for its users. They make it very easy to use and make your account and put your content and start taking payments. But maybe it can take some hours to figure out most of what you need.

Drag and Drop Page Builder

There are very few features that are very useful here. I really like how easy it is to duplicate pages and the fact is I can save templates to use them later again. Their page builder is a very important feature, and they have pulled it off. When you get used to it, it is an easy to use drag and drop page builder.

A/B Testing

With ClickFunnels you will get a free A/B testing tool which is great itself to test your landing pages on various platforms also. All you need to do is duplicate a page and make your changes and choose the traffic you want to see version A or B.

Various Selection of Integrations

ClickFunnels handles API Integrations well. Email and payment processors are not too good but they are working reliably and are easier to manage.

WordPress Integration Plugin

You will also get a WordPress plugin that can let you add ClickFunnels pages to your WordPress website and use it for your marketing. It saves a lot of time and is less than working between ClickFunnels and WordPress without a plugin. The ClickFunnels WordPress Plugin is also rated about 4.4 stars in WordPress Plugin store and has about 20k active installations.

All in one

From ClickFunnels you can build your landing pages, create various forms and order forms, and send marketing emails all from one platform. All of this really does save your time and money, various resources you would otherwise need to spend on many developers, CSS, hosting configurations, and more.

Reasonable Value

If you have more than 20000 visitors and need over 100 pages then ClickFunnels Platinum might not be too much.

ClickFunnels is not perfect but it does a great job of packaging great tools together and making them accessible.

Cons of ClickFunnels

I have some bad news, not much but still, there are few areas that need to work on ClickFunnels. Our review can’t be completed without looking at the ugly side as well.

Overpriced?

A very small drawback is the price. There are only three pricing options like $97/month, $297/month, and $2,497/month. In this tool, this is not a big deal just another investment in your business, and as I have said above you will get a good package for it.

Customer Service

Customer support is good but in my experience, they have been fine with a basic question but if you will ask them more difficult things especially about more advanced features they can’t be very helpful.

Too Templates

This one is not on ClickFunnels but more on the community. While you have the ability to customize with ClickFunnels, many people use similar designs. By considering the popularity of ClickFunnels, this has to be a large amount of ClickFunnels looks good.

Bugs

ClickFunnels will go down or experience some errors. This doesn’t happen any time but it does. If you are investing a lot in advertising and driving traffic to your funnels and a ClickFunnels glitch brings your pages down for even a few hours you can lose a lot of money.

ClickFunnels Pricing – How much is Clickfunnels?

ClickFunnels have two plans which are ClickFunnels Standard Plan and their ClickFunnels Platinum Plan. ClickFunnels is $97 per month and Platinum comes in at $297 per month. And if you want to try all its features you will get a 14 days free trial to try their services, so at the end you don’t need to commit to an expensive plan. If you will pay annually instead of monthly then you will get two months free in $997 for ClickFunnels and $2997 for platinum plan.

If you will compare ClickFunnels with LeadPages, a similar service which starts at $25 per month but it is important to keep in mind about ClickFunnels main service is to create funnels for you with more efficiency and it does a good job in this. I can consider it a good investment.

The main difference between an entry level account and a premium account are :

The standard plan gets the main service the funnel builder with various limitations on the amount of pages, visits and custom domains which is only 3 and only some of the full list features.

But both the plans have unlimited contacts, unlimited members can be added and all the advanced funnels, A/B Split tests and a few other features which can be helpful to create your own  sales funnels.

What is FunnelFlix?

ClickFunnels Page Builder

We can say this is the main feature of ClickFunnels, so I would like to devote special attention to it. ClickFunnels have a solid landing page builder. ClickFunnels distances itself from page building software and advertises its funnel creating software but its landing page builder is also very easy to use and it includes various elements which can convert customers for you easily.

You can also save various templates and make personal page templates and it's very easy to find them, as you can see toggle with basic settings and you can open them in the editor.
