Thrive Themes Vs ClickFunnels: The Ultimate Review

Introduction to Thrive Themes Vs ClickFunnels

If you are aware of lead generations and lead capturing then you will surely hear about ClickFunnels and Thrive Themes.

ClickFunnels is a feature and most used sales funnel concept platform. It also offers various functions including customized funnel templates, membership, and split testing for landing pages.

And,

The Thrive Themes Membership is a complete suite of conversion-focused WordPress themes and plugins and its tools and plugins range from page builders and marketing timers to landing page templates with A/B Testing tools.

So, Simply we can say that ClickFunnels is basically used and designed for creating sales funnels to make your sales and marketing easy and Thrive Membership was the complete suite of marketing and sales which includes WordPress Plugins which make sure to run your marketing landing pages and marketing processes in an easy manner.

And comparing Thrive Membership with ClickFunnels is like comparing Fruits with Vegetables. Both are helpful in creating optimized and conversion-optimized funnels with A/B Testing capabilities.

Thrive themes is not advertised for funnel building solution but funnels you will create with ClickFunnels can also be created with Thrive Themes and its plugins. Both platforms differ in their features, functionality, and price.

So, let’s start the comparison by providing the meaning of the term ‘funnel’.

A funnel is a process of converting leads towards a goad. Funnels can be anything like a set of pages and structured sequences.

As you can see ClickFunnels standard is almost 5 times more expensive than the Thrive Membership.

So what you are paying for with ClickFunnels just to integrate all the funnel steps and processes within the one platform. ClickFunnels standard still requires you to integrate with other third-party programs to use email services and payment and you can configure them within the ClickFunnels dashboard as this is not an all in one software or solution, although Thrive Themes can easily integrate with all the tools needed to create a functioning funnel. With Thrive Themes you will need to go into the third party programs directly to integrate them with Thrive Themes.

Let me explain the difference, for example if you are going to setup a payment page. After connecting your ClickFunnels account to the third party payment gateway such as Strip, you can create a payment page within your ClickFunnels dashboard. Which means you can create and edit your product details and order within the ClickFunnels Dashboard.

And, if you are using thrive themes, then you need to configure your checkout page within the stripe plugin of WordPress then you can link this to your checkout page.

There is an all in one feature for ClickFunnels which includes its hosting too. With ClickFunnels your hosting of pages also included. With Thrive Themes you need a WordPress website which is hosted by yourself.

And as the comparison of the price difference between both, getting your own hosting is not very expensive than buying ClickFunnels for that much cost.

Also the price difference between ClickFunnels standard and ClickFunnels Etison Elite is because Etison Elite includes its email autoresponder, CRM and affiliate management system which is very useful while you are running an online business.

If you want to sell an e-book for example, you need a simple version of this type of funnel can be a long form sales page which links to an order page.

A more advanced funnel can be a free guide which starts an automated email follow up sequence, ending in an offer to purchase the e-book.

Although, Thrive Themes doesn’t include payment systems within the platform. To take payment you need to link to an external payment page or seperate page within your WordPress site that has a payment plugin like Woocommerce setup. This is very easy to do but it can be more difficult when you want to track your actual conversions.

However, ClickFunnels provides a complete incline solution for any purchase focused funnel. You still need to connect with a third party payment system but you can configure the details within the ClickFunnels platform.

Also you can set up one time payments, basic emails and delivery information if required. You can also take payment from within a ClickFunnels page.

Conclusions

If you need a website, a blog or to grow your email list based on the given cost comparison and WordPress integration, the Thrive Membership is the best option. When it comes to funnels, Thrive themes also hold a great place.

With ClickFunnels, you will get a useful top down funnel view. The most determining features of ClickFunnels are inbuilt payment capabilities and membership areas as well as their easy automations. If those things are not that much important in your funnel or you are already using third party programs for those, then Thrive Memberships is a great way of building the same funnels at that fraction of cost.
