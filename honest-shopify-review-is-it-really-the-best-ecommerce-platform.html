What is Shopify?

Shopify is an online application that helps you to create an ecommerce online store. The main aim for creating shopify is to simplify the process for ecommerce beginners and helping them meet their branding requirements. Shopify provides a huge number of ecommerce templates that can be easily customised by the user. The user don't need to enter in the technicalities of designing and coding a website neither need to hire a web designing agency or a developer. However for people who know HTML and CSS, shopify provides you with both the features. You can customize the coding of the template to some extent.

The best part of using shopify is that you don't have to worry about buying a web hosting service or installing softwares on the server. Or I can say everything that you need to build and run your online store is being maintained out of your reach. So no need to worry about it. All you need is your product product and its pictures. Shopify quoted on their website that around 325,000 people are using shopify, and have generated a total of $24bn in sales.
