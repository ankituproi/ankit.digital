Top 10 Best WordPress Hosting Services in 2020

Are you looking for best web hosting for your WordPress site? Here in this article we will help you to choose a better web host for your website.

We all know choosing the right hosting for your WordPress website is much important for your rankings and speed also. Your WordPress site’s health always relies on the hosting provider you use for your website.

But how you can choose a better WordPress host?

There are many web hosting providers to choose from like Bluehost, SiteGround, HostGator..etc.

There are three very important factors for WordPress hosting:

  • Speed: Load Time
  • Uptime: Uptime of any website server should be at least 99% because of the ranking of any WordPress website.
  • Customer Support: Customer support should answer your questions related to your WordPress website.

Usually, we can’t guarantee you will get similar uptime or speed for your website as this varies on different factors like your chosen hosting plan, your website size, and the number of visitors coming to your website.

Let’s start with the best hosting providers first then we will move down to the list to see which one you have to avoid or choose. Here are the top 10 web hostings for WordPress:

1. Bluehost (www.bluehost.com) 

  • Uptime: 99.98%
  • Load time: 553 ms
  • Support: 24/7 live chat, knowledge base, phone
  • Features: One-click install for WordPress, free SSL, free domain for 1 year
  • Website: www.Bluehost.com

Bluehost is a very popular hosting service among many small business owners and bloggers who use WordPress for their website. The simple reason is- they have cheap prices starting from $2.75/month and it’s simple to use like one-click WordPress installation option and they usually don’t have any performance issues.

Also as we have used it for our WordPress websites and we have seen that this is the best cost/value ratio. Our uptime and speed monitoring tool Pingdom showed us an average uptime of our website is 99.98% and an average page loading speed of 554 ms for the last 10-months.

They are also one of the few web hosts that are officially being recommended by WordPress.org. As all of their plans come with free SSL, a WordPress staging environment and a very easy WordPress install feature.

And also Bluehost doesn’t offer a free site migration service with its lowest plan and it will cost you about $500 for transferring anyone site. If you will purchase their plus plan or above then you will get this feature for free for one website.

Bluehost has a very large database for WordPress tutorials and all plans come with 24/7 live support which is very fast and reliable.

Bluehost Pricing

Pricing of Bluehost starts at $2.75/month (with 3 years plan) and they provide a free domain with SSL for a year. Their renewal price is $7.99/month. Bluehost also comes with a one-month money-back guarantee.

Pros of Bluehost:

Strong uptime and load time, free custom domain name and recommended by WordPress.org.

Cons of Bluehost:

No option to pay monthly, no free migration for a website with the lowest plan.

2. HostGator Managed WordPress (www.HostGator.com)

  • Uptime: 99.97%
  • Load time: 284 ms
  • Support: 24/7 live chat, knowledge base, phone
  • Features: Free site transfer and backups. WordPress optimized servers, unlimited bandwidth
  • Website: www.HostGator.com

HostGator hosts almost 15 million domains over the world making them one of the most popular web hosting service providers on the market. Similarly the Bluehost, HostGator also offers WordPress hosting solutions.

HostGator was the best option for high traffic websites which needs to be good at loading speed. HostGator WordPress plan can also make your website faster across different regions and for mobile users.

All of their plans have unlimited bandwidth, storage, free email accounts, and a free site migration feature.

You can also reach their support any time via phone or live chat and believe us their customer support is very helpful and fast.

HostGator Pricing

Their pricing starting from $5.97/month if you will go with 3 years plan and their renewal price are higher at $9.95/month. Even HostGator offers a 45 days money-back guarantee.

Pros of HostGator

The good Loading speed of the websites, reliable and one-click install WordPress with 1 free site migration option.

Cons of HostGator:

Its renewal price is very high.

3. Hostinger (www.Hostinger.com)

  • Uptime: 99.96%
  • Load time: 308 ms
  • Support: 24/7 live chat, knowledge base
  • Features: Free email account, unlimited bandwidth, free SSL
  • Website: www.Hostinger.com

Hostinger is the hosting service provider from Europe but has data centers in the USA, Asia, and Europe. They don’t have any option to choose the data center but once you are signed up you can ask them to change your data centers from the USA to Asia or Europe.

They also have a WordPress builder which is not very ideal but it can help in the beginning a lot. Hostinger also offers 24/7 live chat support which is very helpful and they also have the largest library of WordPress guides.

Hostinger Pricing

Their pricing for WordPress starts from $2.15/Month if you choose 4 years time period and it will renew at $3.49/Month. This plan also includes unlimited bandwidth and 20GB disc space with 1 email account as well as an SSL Certificate and free site migration option.

And also they will provide you 30 days refund guarantee from the signup if you are not satisfied with their services.

Pros of Hostinger

  • Cheap Price
  • One-Click WordPress install
  • Fast Speed
  • Free Site Migration

Cons of Hostinger

  • Average Uptime
  • Very Limited Disc Space for all of their plan.

4. SiteGround (www.siteground.com)

  • Uptime: 99.99%
  • Load time: 643 ms
  • Support: 24/7 live chat
  • Features: Google cloud servers, free https (SSL), free email, free site migration
  • Website: www.SiteGround.com 

SiteGround is also the most popular WordPress hosting service provider and recommended by Wordpress.org itself. From 2004, SiteGround started and growing as a large network of about 800,000 domain names and hosts those around the world.

They are in the list of few hosts who provide you an option to change your data center locations from Europe to the USA, the USA to Europe. So if your visitors are mainly from the USA only it’s a better option for you.

Also, they offer WordPress site migration for free which also includes a free CDN and image optimization service which makes your WordPress website faster than before.

Their Customer support was also very friendly and helpful and they answer your questions too quickly with very less waiting time to connect with their support via live chat and you can also call them directly but waiting time in call support is longer than live chat support and you will get a good experience with their support team.

SiteGround Pricing

In terms of pricing, SiteGround is expensive. Their pricing starts from $6.99/month with a 12-month plan but it renews at $14.99/month without any free domain but they offer free email accounts.

Additionally, they offer a 1-month money-back guarantee.

Pros of SiteGround

  • Good Uptime
  • Free Site Migration
  • Fast Speed
  • Recommended by Woocommerce and WordPress.org
  • Free SSL

Cons of SiteGround

  • High Prices without any free domain

5. A2 Hosting (www.A2Hosting.com)

  • Uptime: 99.98%
  • Load time: 303 ms
  • Support: 24/7 live chat, phone, knowledge base, phone
  • Features: Fast servers, free SSL, free CDN, unlimited bandwidth
  • Website: www.A2Hosting.com

A2 Hosting is another low budget WordPress hosting. All of their plans come with SSL (Let’s Encrypt), free Cloudflare CDN, and unlimited bandwidth, and 100GB SSD Storage. Same like Siteground, you can also get to change data center locations when you want. They currently offer data centers in the USA, Netherland, and Singapore.

But as much as their services better their Customer support isn’t that much ideal it can take up to 8 minutes to connect and available 24/7. In addition, to live chat, you can also connect with them via phone too.

Also,

According to performance tests, A2 Hosting was 2nd most fastest shared WordPress hosting provider at 303 ms. A2 Hosting’s average uptime has been 99.98% with 2 hours of downtime per year.

A2 Hosting Pricing

Their pricing starts from $2.99/month for minimum registration of 3 years and it will be renewed at $8.99/month. You can also get free unlimited email accounts and website migrations and 100GB of disc space. If you own many WordPress websites then A2 Hosting was the better convenient host for you.

One plus point is A2 Offers any time money-back guarantee.

Pros of A2 Hosting

  • Fast hosting for WordPress.
  • Optimized servers for WordPress
  • Free Site Migration
  • Anytime Money back guarantee.

Cons of A2 Hosting

Renewal prices are too high.

6. GreenGeeks (www.GreenGeeks.com)

  • Uptime: 99.95%
  • Load time: 434 ms
  • Support: 24/7 live chat, phone, knowledge base
  • Features: Unlimited storage & bandwidth, automatic updates, free SSL
  • Website: www.GreenGeeks.com

If you are looking for fast, secure, and scalable Web Hosting Providers for WordPress then GreenGeeks is suitable for you. Their name itself means that they have been implementing any environment-friendly approach to hosting websites.

All of the plans of GreenGeeks include free SSL, free CDN, unlimited email accounts, unlimited bandwidth, free WordPress site transfer, and unlimited storage. They offer three data centers which are the USA, Europe, and Canada. And when you sign up, you have an option to choose your data center or server location.

If we look for support from GreenGeeks, then they provide support from Live Chat, email, and phone support as well as WordPress related tutorials and guides.

In terms of performance, they are providing a speed of 434ms which is good, and reliable uptime of 99.95%.

GreenGeeks Pricing

Their plans start from $2.95/month which renews at $9.95/month. They provide a 30-days money-back guarantee but if you have taken a free domain offer then they will deduct $13.95 for a domain name registration fee.

Pros of GreenGeeks

  • Good speed
  • Green hosting provider
  • Free SSL
  • Free site migration

Cons of GreenGeeks

  • High Renewal Cost

7. InMotion (www.InMotionHosting.com)

  • Uptime: 99.92%
  • Load time: 598 ms
  • Support: 24/7 live chat, phone, knowledge base
  • Features: WordPress is pre-installed, unlimited bandwidth, unlimited email accounts
  • Website: www.InMotionHosting.com

InMotion hosting is also the most popular WordPress hosting service provider which is US-based and with more than 350,000 domain names over the world.

There are many reasons which make InMotion a good fit for WordPress users. Every plan comes with free SSL, CDN, unlimited email accounts, and more than 200 free WordPress themes. They provide a limited number of sites to be hosted and disk space for all of those.

They provide fast and reliable 24/7 live chat and phone support based in the US. In addition to these, free WordPress transfer and they have their own BoldGrid pre-installed website builder which makes you build your website itself.

Uptime and speed of InMotion Hosting are average, but enough. They have 99.92% uptime and an average speed of 598 ms for the last 12 months as we have noticed.

InMotion Pricing

They have various WordPress plans which start from $4.99/month with a 3-year contract and it renews at $7.99/month that may vary from websites, storage, and bandwidth. They also provide dedicated WordPress solutions. Also, they offer a 90- days money-back guarantee.

Pros of InMotion

  • Good customer support

Cons of InMotion

  • Uptime is average.

Site5 is not that much cheaper shared WordPress hosting provider but they claim to be the best option for developers as they offer a flexible control panel, good customer support, and stable hosting.

Similarly to others, Site5 also offers free Cloudflare CDN, 27/7 live chat support and site transfers. They provide 99.9% uptime guarantee. But you can’t choose data centers and default server location is set to the USA (Central).

Cons of Site5

9. DreamHost (www.DreamHost.com)

  • Uptime: 99.63%
  • Load time: 894 ms
  • Support: knowledge base, limited live chat
  • Features: WordPress is pre-installed, drag and drop builder, unlimited bandwidth/storage
  • Website: www.DreamHost.com

As much we know DreamHost is a very popular and well established hosting service provider from the USA with over 340,000 customers. Just like Bluehost and Siteground, they are also recommended by WordPress.org.

Their Goal is so simple which is - to be fast, reliable, and optimized for WordPress users.

All of their plans (Shared WordPress, Managed WordPress, and VPS) offer free WordPress website builder, free SSL and SSD Storage and Unlimited Bandwidth so your website can’t be slowed down when your visitors increase to your website.

They don't offer free site transfers, it costs $99 but they provide this for free when you select a plan of $16.95/month and they also do not offer a 24/7 support option. They have live chat support, but it is only available from 5:30AM to 9:30 PM Pacific time but at least it's 7 days a week.

They are also not an ideal host for a website that servers visitors outside the USA or Asia. And their uptime is only 99.63% with an page loading speed of 894 ms. You can’t select server locations outside the USA.

DreamHost Pricing

Their plans start from $2.59/month with 3-year commitment or $4.95/month. DreamHost also has high performance managed WordPress hosting plans which are more expensive than others which costs $16.95/month. Their email accounts cost $19.99/year and free domain for a year which also renews at $15.99/year. DreamHosts gives a 97-days money-back guarantee.

Pros of DreamHost

  • Monthly billing available

  • Average Uptime
  • Free SSL
  • Free WordPress website builder

Cons of DreamHost

  • Too Costly
  • Email accounts are not free
  • Renewal cost also high
  • No option to choose data centers outside the USA.
  • Limited Live Chat option

10. iPage (www.iPage.com)

  • Uptime: 99.80%
  • Load time: 799 ms
  • Support: 24/7 live chat, phone, knowledge base
  • Features: Free domain, unlimited storage & bandwidth, free SSL
  • Website: www.iPage.com

iPage has basically two WordPress plans. Both plans offer a free domain name for 1 year with one-click install as well as a free SSL Certificate. Their main focus is to provide the best experience from registration to customer support. And all of the plans had both unlimited bandwidth and unlimited storage.

iPage doesn’t provide any CDN, you can’t change data centers, you have to use the default datacenter which is the USA only and there are no free site transfers too.

They also offer live chat support and phone support for 24/7 and also there is a knowledge base.

They provide uptime of 99.8% and loading time of 799 ms which are not good as most of the other WordPress web hosts above and their performance is lacking.

iPage Pricing

Their plans cost starting from $3.75/month for 1-3 years. Monthly and yearly pack renews at $9.49/month, a 2 years plan renews at $8.49/month and a 3 year plan renews at $7.49/month. Their backups cost $1/month and email accounts are only through Google suite $6 per month. They also offer a 30-days money-back guarantee for all of their plans.

Pros of iPage

  • Best apps on the web

Cons of iPage

WordPress Hosting Summary

We have provided a detailed review of the top 10 wordpress hosting providers. Our each review was based on speed, uptime and costs which we have checked ourselves on a test website by testing it on pingdom which is used to monitor and analyze performance.

We recommend using a web hosting provider that provides you an uptime of minimum 99.94% and loading time lesser than 700 ms.
