Top 5 Live Chat Softwares To Boost Your Conversions

Live chat software has become the most popular point of contact for managing customer support in easy for and also with live chat option customer support and online sales-related activities are much easy. A website with live chat software, real-time monitoring, built-in ticketing systems, data collection can be done with live chat software but only when you choose the correct and best live chat software for your website. So, if you are running a customer-oriented business or website, then for customer support, live chat software can be very helpful for your business to provide immediate customer support.

So for choosing the best live chat software for your business website or eCommerce website, here in this article, I will let you know about the top 5 live chat software with pricing, features, pros, and cons.

Top Live Chat Softwares

  1. LiveChat
  2. HubSpot Live Chat
  3. ChatBot
  4. Gist
  5. LiveAgent

Let’s Start,

LiveChat

LiveChat is the best solution for e-commerce businesses that want to build long-lasting relationships with their customers.

What is LiveChat?

LiveChat is the top live chat software eCommerce store and something advanced customer services. It is a complete customer service that is dedicated to eCommerce that can boost conversions and provide analytics for the website.

This platform is the alternative to time-consuming and expensive phone calls and emails that can take much longer than the immediate live chat responses.

LiveChat Pricing

There are four plans of LiveChat that can help your website to get the best customer support and boost your conversions.

HubSpot Live Chat

Live Chat software of HubSpot is one of the best free live chat software and the perfect solution for those already using Hubspot Products.

What is HubSpot Live Chat?

HubSpot’s live chat product gives sales, marketing, customer support teams a universal inbox to view, reply, and manage every interaction of customers means you can find everything in one place.

This software allows everyone to connect their website visitors automatically with the right person on their team to increase conversion rates and close more deals with better customer service.

HubSpot Live Chat Pricing

Live Chat software of HubSpot comes with a free plan as well with some limitations or number of contacts you can chat. Their pricing levels are categorized with free, starter, professional, and enterprise plans that cost $0, $46/month, $800/month, and $3200/month respectively.

Cons of HubSpot Live Chat

  • Limited integrations
