Thrive Landing Pages & Content Builder Review 2017

So you have already heard the hype about Thrive Landing Pages & Content Builder, but want to get a taste of it before spending on it? Alright then...

Well as soon as Thrive released the 1st version, I had my hands on the plugin after some of my friends told me about how feature loaded, value for money it is and how they have generated so many conversions after employing the plugin. Plus they don't have to replace their beautiful looking themes which are generally not good at conversions. Everything happens like magic with this plugin.

Here, I will be sharing my honest review (2017) & feedback so that you can take a look at the options & features available out of the box which will help you decide whether to go for it or not. I strongly suggest it though.

Lets begin then.....

What is Thrive Landing Pages & Content Builder?

Thrive Landing Pages & Content Builder is an awesome WordPress plugin which is drag & drop, high conversion page builder and editor created by Thrive themes keeping in mind & strictly adhering to the rule "What you see is what you get", - "Huh, what did he just say?". What I mean is you can see the webpage while you are editing it exactly the way audience will see it.

Webpage is displayed right away to me, while editing as it will look to you

Editing the same post in default WordPress editor

Now, are there any advantages of using Thrive editor as compared to traditional WordPress editor?- Yes.. lots of them like:

  1. Knowledge of HTML or CSS is no more required to create complex, beautiful & brilliantly performing webpage layouts.
  2. Even if someone is well versed with HTML or CSS, it makes the process of creating the webpages a lot faster & easier.
  3. 102+ templates are available to choose from to create high conversion landing pages. These templates are categorized in sets for best webpage development experience.
  4. You can create 2 step opt-in forms where a popover is triggered by some action. This generally gives better results as compared to just displaying an opt-in form traditional way.
  5. You get so many options that you can create almost any type of webpage without the need of any other plugin.

"My money will give me more than expected"- you are right buddy. Now lets see what features are there for you right away.

How to install and use Thrive landing Pages & Content Builder?

Installation of Thrive Lead Pages

To get started, first you need to download the plugin as a zip folder (.zip) & upload it to WordPress plugin area. Then you need to click on Activate to make the plugin go live & ready to use.

However, if you are using the plugin for the first time then it will ask you to validate the licence as in above image. Here, enter the e-mail address you used while purchasing the plugin in the 1st field & the corresponding licence in second field. Click on Activate Licence. Huraaah.... All done. You can now start editing your 1st webpage.

Now whether you are creating a new post or just want to improvise an old post, click on Edit Using Thrive Content Builder if you want to use Thrive as your editor.

You can now start editing your post with all the Thrive Pages options on the right hand side. The more creative you will be with the webpage creation & editing process, the more awesome will be the webpages and that's how you get appreciation from audience in the form of likes, comments & sharing of the post.

Following are the options available in the Thrive Content Builder.

The last section "Thrive Theme Elements" will only be available, if you use themes created by Thrive.

This is all we can say, we get in Thrive Page Builder with all the options listed in a scroll-able vertical bar but hold on, this is not just a small list. It has got all the elements you will ever need to create any kind of webpage. We will discuss these elements in detail later in this post.

You can just click on an element or an drag & drop it on the webpage, to add it to webpage. The element can be further customized using element specific options which will be available after you click on that element.

At the top of this bar, you will find the most important features like Save Changes button, Undo & Redo icons, Preview button, HTML icon & Templates.

There is also one drop down menu that say Thrive Landing Pages available which will give you the choice of  3 different styles to choose from:

  1. Flat
  2. Classy
  3. Minimal

I will elaborate this option in detail in later section of the post.

There are also other options available to facilitate ease of use of the plugin like you can collapse the menu, reverse the color of editor & change the placement of the editor on the left hand side.

Thrive Landing Pages Builder is so loaded with features, that everything is just available out of the box. Plus it is very easy to use and  the drag & drop works so seamlessly while eliminating the delay in updating the webpage, as if you are using the drag & drop on a desktop application.

Whats Included?

Apart from basic editing options, images, texts available in generic WordPress Editor, there are a lot of wonderful options included in Content Builder. To list a few of them, here they are:

Buttons: You can easily create & edit beautiful looking buttons. These can be further customized highly using available button options like links (whether to open these in same window or new window), whether to use the button as event manager for conversions (in case of Leadpages), etc.

Social Media: Add Click to Tweet for Twitter social media involvement in any webpage to increase your social bookmarking technique of off-page SEO.

Count-down Timers: Want to convert users into potential leads quickly, then utilize this to incorporate countdown timer with desired date & time frame, text to show after time completion, to make them feel a sense of limit, urgency. This is very useful & one of the most important feature found in most of the Leadpages these days.

Google Maps: You can embed Google maps right in the webpage to inform user about a lot of things like Branches, directions, etc. This is the one of the must use feature in Contact pages.

Opt-In forms: This is a fairly new feature to involve potential leads where a popover is used to display opt-in forms after clicking a button with some involving text. This in general generate more leads & is beautiful as compared to displaying a plain opt-in form.

Pricing Tables: So you have different prices for different products/services you offer & want to generate Leads. However in that case, user need to decide for which package he/she want to opt for. The answer to this is pricing tables and these are very easy to create & edit in Thrive Leadpages & Content Builder. Just drag & drop the pricing table element and fill in the information according to your needs. You are ready to roll.

Tables: You can also create different form of tables in your webpages using Thrive to compare data.

Money Back Guarantee: A money back guarantee offer gives user sense of trust, authority & reality plus freedom to choose while opting for a product/service. This is one of the feature a landing page must have to really attract users. Customized money back guarantee offers can also be generated using Thrive Landing Pages & Content Builder.

Testimonials: Your landing page testimonials is another factor that gain users trust immediately. You can generate 9 different style testimonials using Thrive that stand out from other landing pages.

Star ratings: Star ratings is very attractive for a user who considers testimonials or other user reviews seriously before opting for a particular product/service. This can be embedded in a webpage using Thrive very easily & can play a very important role in attracting users.

Apart from the elements I have discussed in detail above, you can also add other elements which are self descriptive & listed below:

  1. Custom CSS
  2. Custom HTML
  3. Table of contents
  4. Content boxes
  5. Responsive videos
  6. Tabbed content
  7. Dividers
  8. Styled lists

You can say there are even more. It all depends on how you visualize & use features & Thrive is continuously adding more & more features.

Some Of The Most Important Features Explained

Landing Page Templates

Thrive Content Builder is all about making beautiful pages/posts from scratch.... NO, its even more. Now can instantly covert a page/post into a landing page without manually dragging & dropping elements by using the "Thrive Landing Pages" drop down which is available at the very top of the Thrive bar.

This feature was not available in the initial release of the builder, which is then included in later versions after continuous user requests & suggestions.

You can choose your landing page from this dialog-box.

Now say you have switched your page/post into a landing page. If you go to the same drop-down menu, you will find more settings:

These options will allow you to reset you landing page back to default post or theme, choose another landing page or make changes to current landing page settings.

New Event Manger Allow 2 Step Opt-In forms

Thrive's event manager allows you to create 2 step opt-in forms using animations which can be applied on buttons, images & a couple of other elements. These type of opt-in forms grabs audience attention immediately & is best for building an email list.

So to setup a 2 step opt-in form on a particular element (say button), you need to add the element first & then click on the event manager.

You will now be able to add an event by using thrive light-box to be displayed when the button will be clicked (we can also display light-box on other user actions).

Then you need to select the light-box to display & the corresponding animation.

Save the event after the desired changes have been made to the event. Then save the page & preview it by clicking the button to show the popover.

That's it.. NO, there's more.

Now assume you have used the same light-box on multiple pages and you want to edit the light-box. For this one don't need to edit all the light-boxes one by one. You can just edit the light-box out of the page & it will be updated on all pages automatically.

Head over to the Thrive Light-boxes section & select "Edit Thrive Content Builder" & make the necessary changes.

Style Families

This is that one factor that matters from user point of view the most i.e the looks of the website. Thrive have taken care of this by offering three different style families which are:

  1. Flat
  2. Classy
  3. Minimal

Once you choose a style from the list. it will be applied to the whole theme & the elements accordingly.

Highly Customization Of Colors Margins

This Content Builder has won again by allowing us to customize the look & feel of the website to another level. While most of the builders allow us to just indent a particular element left, right or center while we can do nothing about the white space in between elements. Hurrah no more the same thing.

Thrive Content Builder also allows you to input margins plus padding's which gives us more control on spacing between the elements in addition to the elements.

Additionally, color of each element can be highly customized to match the look & feel of your website.

Short-code's Usage

Other drag & drop editor does not support native WP short-codes at all. As a result, 3rd party plugins become hard to use & that's bad.

Thrive on the other hand lets you do the same & also updates it in real-time to show you the result while editing, awesome.. Right.

Others

In addition to above features, it supports WordPress revision manager which is very useful when you have done a mistake or want to review older revisions of your content to analyse it. It also supports custom page level CSS & HTML which comes handy to give finishing touch to your content & look of website.

This is not a promotion review where you all see just spend $69 & that's it, nothing else needed.

Read, think, decide whether you really need what Thrive has to offer you and if the answer is YES... Go FOR IT.
