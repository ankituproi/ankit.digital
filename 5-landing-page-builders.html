5 Landing Page Builders

Landing Pages - You can call it the lifeline to convert your target leads to potential customers. It's the key to Conversion.

A Digital Marketing Campaign is lifeless and unplanned without a Landing Page. Marketing is all about converting those targeted leads to lifelong potential customers. Without Landing page, your visitors might get confused about what to do next. Where to take the step? Where to see the details of what made them click on your ad?

Honestly, generic homepages can never serve your purpose of lead conversion. It needs a particular page that pinpoints all the specific consumer needs. That's how they will convert.

Unfortunately, a landing page doesn't build up on its own.

But, you don't need to run after the coders to design it from scratch.

Trust me, its pain.

So what to do?

The good thing is there are plenty of tools out there to make the whole process of creation & management more comfortable for you, and you can create them in a fraction of time. A single landing page might not serve your purpose, for a successful campaign, you need to experiment with multiple designs and lead form placement within the landing page. So managing and optimizing your set of landing pages for better performance, it's pretty well become your full-time job until you have a single dashboard that helps you to optimize and manage effectively.

Landing page builders can save your precious time & money as well.

{But before moving forward with the landing page builders, let's understand the difference between a landing page and a home page and why they are so important.

1. Elementor

Starting with the best one which I have frequently been using.

Elementor is a WordPress plugin with the drag & drop feature. It provides you a wholly original and separate interface for creating posts, custom post types pages. So when you open a standard format of creating posts or pages, you get a new button that allows you to enable the elementor, and then its a completely different and straightforward page editing process.

The best thing it can be done through a handy user interface, and you don't need to have any prior knowledge or skills about HTML or PHP. As it works on the frontend, it will show you the outcome right away so that you can visualize where it is going to stand. You can create any layout for your page regardless of what your current WordPress theme allows you to do. Also, it can quickly gel up with all the WordPress themes.

You can create impressive content structure and page layouts, even if you are not a designer or doesn't have site building coding skills. As per the latest report by elementor, they claim that their plugin has been downloaded for more than a million times.

If you are not willing to create a page from scratch, you can pick one from their pool of ready to use templates and tweak them a little to get your desired output.

Pricing
